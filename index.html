SAFELY HOME

We provide a hands free way to get you home in the dark. With text to speech we'll guide you via the brightest and busiest route without the need for you to look at your phone.

What we do

Candle Path has been made with a host of features to keep you safe on your way home.

1. Brightest route home

Candle Path will plot the safest route home by looking at available light street data. The brighter the route the safer you are, this application is ideal for anyone new to an area or for those who have had a few too many.

2. Text to speech navigation

Using OpenEars Candle Path reads you the directions and navigates you home without the need to expose your phone. Played through headphones this ensures undesirables don't get to see your possessions.

3. Emergency Monitoring

For a one off fee per night Candle Path will enable Emergency Monitoring for your trip home. If you or your phone stray from the route in question, an emergency text is sent to a person of your choice with details of your location and your need for help.

4. Pay with PayPal

Seamless PayPal integration removes the need for cash or card, pay with your email address before you set home to ensure peace of mind for you or a friend.

5. Native iOS

Built for iPhone 5 using native code and flat design, this application is quick and easy to use.

6. Thermal printing at home

With a small and convenient thermal printing kit, arduino uno and a home computer you can get a receipt of your route home.
