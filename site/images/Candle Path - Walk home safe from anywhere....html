Walk home safe from anywhere...

Candle Path uses amazing techonolgy & open data to get you from A to B, using the safest routes.

Perfect for after a night out or leaving work late.

Safest Route Home


Candle Path will plot the safest route home by looking at a variety of data from street lights, crime rates and population trends. The application is ideal for anyone new to the area or for those who have had a few too many.

Emeregency Monitoring


For a one off fee per night Candle Path will enable Emergency Monitoring for your trip home. If you or your phone stray from the route in question, an emergency text is sent to a person of your choice with details of your location and your need for help.

Easy Crime Reporting Tool


With Candle Path's crime reporter tool, people can report incidents & your path will change in real-time. Nothing can beat real people working together.
